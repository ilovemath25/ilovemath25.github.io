GULU GULU

Gulu-Gulu is a game I created as part of my first-ever game competition, in collaboration with my sister. My sister handled the design, while I handled the coding part. It's a pixel-art adventure where players can change its size, navigate rooms, solve puzzles, and interact with their surroundings. This project challenged me to combine coding, design, storytelling, creative problem solving, and teamwork.
